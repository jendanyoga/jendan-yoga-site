What is JenDan Yoga?

JenDan Yoga is a modern flow practice that blends traditional yoga movement with Chinese meridian energy work. Through mindful motion, breath, and gentle energetic cleansing techniques, the practice supports the body's natural energy pathways.

By helping the meridian system flow more freely, JenDan Yoga encourages balance throughout the entire energy body, supporting physical wellbeing, emotional clarity, and overall whole-body health.

Each session is designed to leave you feeling lighter, more aligned, and deeply connected to your body's natural rhythm.
